USERS notes:

  • The droid has a hybrid Kinematic setup - which means that some of the droid's body is controlled by Inverse Kinematics and other parts are controlled by regular Forward Kinematics (i.e. Keyframing)
  • To pose the arms drag and rotate the **Left Arm Goal** & **Right Arm Goal**
  • To pose the legs drag and/or rotate the **Left Leg Goal** and/or the **Right Leg Goal**
  • The feet are left for Manual Keyframing -- adjust the feet objects directly
  • To move the entire droid around in the scene manipulate the **MASTER MOVER** null
  • To pose the head and neck manipulate the **Head Goal** ---- to rotate the head again manipulate the same goal - it will just affect the head object and not the neck.
  • I have not yet had time to work out a effective solution to keep the palm of the left hand parented to the foregrip of the gun object when posing in the appropriate scene file - if any of you can do it please let me know and I will incorporate it into the release pack :o)
  • Walk cycles are included for the various ranked droids

Please note that although the walk cycle is lovely and smooth in 6.5 the resultant export and less advanced IK engine in 5.6 causse some awkward snapping of the left leg joint - I tried to fix it but decided to leave it as is - so you have been warned :o)
